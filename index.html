Our Story

KIDAYO (Kampala Inter-District Adventist Youth Organisation) is a vibrant ministry dedicated to inspiring and equipping young Adventist leaders in Uganda.

Since our establishment, we've been spreading the gospel through quality music, community service, and meaningful fellowship. Our mission is to lift the name of Jesus and share His good news through various youth-focused initiatives.

We believe in empowering young people to become agents of positive change in their communities while deepening their faith and spiritual growth.
